for now we can just stick to “simple sweet spot”

the main point of the app is to help NSA runners visualize and adjust their run and sched, and to journal

how to use the app?

the runner adds a recent PR (should indicate that the PR should be recent and not 1 year ago, because current fitness is important)

whether 5k or up to 42k, maybe whatever distance, we should just calculate from this

additionally, it is highly recommended to enter the max HR (should be get from the specific workout)

then we now have the “baseline sweet spot workouts”

3 x 10’ @ recentPR(5k pace, todo: get the formula from the book)

5 x 7’

10 x 3’

then a suggested easy pace range

this creates a week schedule

the quality workouts, the easy runs, and the long run

depends on the day today, it should show the workout of the day

in the workout of the day, there should be options to tweak

what i mean is, there are sliders for temperature, the wind, the humidity, and all the other variables discussed in the book + toggle of trail if concrete or asphalt + etc.

then how is the runner feeling pre-run? 1-10, this should be answered because this is one of the variables where it is measured subjectively

then there will be a formulated suggested workout of the day, adjusted by the tweaks so that you hit that quality workout with less fatigue, getting closer to the optimal workout, the formulated suggested workout can be toggled off, so that the runner can see the simplified version (just the default pace ranges from the “baseline sweet spot workouts”

then post run, we can just upload the run like after the tweaks, if you followed and ran the suggested workout, just hit submit, then it will enter a journal entry of the day, then you have these information for you. there should be a note somewhere where runners can add their notes

and maybe photos attached (for later)

when you do this everyday, you will get a journal view like Year 2026, where you see the progression you made, how you look like when it is summer/winter or any season

should be stored in the cloud, people should be logging in with their email, setup profile > enter max hr and recent race
